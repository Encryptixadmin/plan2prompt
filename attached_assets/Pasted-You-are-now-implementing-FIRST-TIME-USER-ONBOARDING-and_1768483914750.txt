You are now implementing FIRST-TIME USER ONBOARDING and EXPECTATION SETTING.

OBJECTIVE:
Ensure users understand the platform’s structured, sequential workflow
BEFORE they encounter any gated or blocked states.

This onboarding is NOT a tutorial.
It is an expectation primer.

--------------------------------
CORE MESSAGE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
--------------------------------
The platform works best when used sequentially:
Idea → Requirements → Build Prompts.

Each step exists to protect the quality of the next.

--------------------------------
WHEN ONBOARDING APPEARS
--------------------------------
Show onboarding ONLY when:
- A user has no existing artefacts
- Or on their very first visit

It must be dismissible and non-intrusive.

--------------------------------
ONBOARDING STRUCTURE
--------------------------------

STEP 1 — WHAT THIS PLATFORM DOES

Title:
"From idea to build-ready prompts"

Body:
"This platform helps you turn an idea into clear, executable build instructions.

It does this in three deliberate steps:
• Clarifying the idea
• Defining exact requirements
• Generating step-by-step build prompts"

--------------------------------

STEP 2 — WHY THE PLATFORM IS SEQUENTIAL

Title:
"Why the steps matter"

Body:
"Each step removes ambiguity from the previous one.

Clear ideas produce better requirements.
Clear requirements produce reliable build prompts.

Skipping steps increases guesswork and reduces quality — so the platform protects the sequence."

--------------------------------

STEP 3 — HOW USERS SHOULD THINK ABOUT PROGRESS

Title:
"Work through artefacts, not pages"

Body:
"You’re not just filling in forms.

Each step creates a reusable artefact:
• An idea reference
• A requirements document
• A set of build prompts

You can stop, review, and resume at any point."

--------------------------------

STEP 4 — SETTING EXPECTATIONS

Title:
"What this platform won’t do"

Body:
"This platform won’t:
• Auto-build your app
• Hide decisions
• Rush you forward
• Lock you into one tool

It’s designed to help you think clearly and build with confidence."

--------------------------------
CTA

UX RULES
--------------------------------
- Use calm, professional language
- No animations
- No progress bars
- No gamification
- No AI hype language

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
--------------------------------

FINAL CHECK

- Users understand sequencing BEFORE hitting enforcement
