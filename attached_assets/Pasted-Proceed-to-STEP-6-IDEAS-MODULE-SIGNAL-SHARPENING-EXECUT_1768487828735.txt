Proceed to STEP 6 — IDEAS MODULE SIGNAL SHARPENING.

EXECUTION MODE:
Judgement quality improvement.
No new UI flows.
No new modules.

OBJECTIVE:
Make idea validation more decisive, honest, and useful
by sharpening signals and reducing encouragement bias.

--------------------------------
CORE PRINCIPLE
--------------------------------
The Ideas Module exists to prevent bad ideas from becoming expensive builds.

--------------------------------
IDEA OUTPUT ENHANCEMENTS
--------------------------------

Enhance idea validation output to ALWAYS include:

--------------------------------
1. CONFIDENCE SCORE WITH RATIONALE
--------------------------------
- Numerical score (0–100)
- Clear explanation of WHY this score was assigned
- Avoid vague language

--------------------------------
2. PRIMARY RISK DRIVERS (RANKED)
--------------------------------
- Top 3–5 risks, ordered by impact
- Each risk must include:
  - Why it matters
  - What would trigger failure
  - Whether it is controllable

--------------------------------
3. SCOPE & COMPLEXITY WARNINGS
--------------------------------
- Explicit callouts where scope may be underestimated
- Highlight hidden complexity (tech, UX, ops, compliance)

--------------------------------
4. ASSUMPTION DEPENDENCY SNAPSHOT
--------------------------------
- List the top assumptions the idea depends on
- Indicate which assumptions are:
  - Validated
  - Unvalidated
  - Risky

--------------------------------
5. FAILURE MODE NARRATIVE
--------------------------------
Include a short section titled:
"How this idea is most likely to fail"

This must be:
- Direct
- Specific
- Non-alarmist

--------------------------------
QUALITY BAR
--------------------------------
A user should be able to decide:
- “Proceed”
- “Revise”
- “Stop”
based on this output alone.

--------------------------------
DO NOT
--------------------------------
- Add market size calculations
- Add business model templates
- Add diagrams or visuals
- Add encouragement language

--------------------------------
REVIEW CHECKPOINT
--------------------------------
After implementation:
- Show before/after idea validation output
- Explain how false positives are reduced
- Confirm no changes to sequencing or gating logic
