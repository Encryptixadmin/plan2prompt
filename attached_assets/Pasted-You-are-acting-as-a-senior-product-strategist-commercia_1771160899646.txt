You are acting as a senior product strategist, commercial analyst, and technical writer.

Your task is to produce a structured Product Summary and Target Market Profile for the current application.

All products must be clearly positioned as built, owned, and operated by MTE Software Ltd.

Do not use marketing hype.
Do not invent features.
Base everything strictly on the implemented functionality and real platform capabilities.

The output must be suitable for:
• Website product pages
• Investor materials
• Partnership discussions
• Capability decks
• Commercial proposals

────────────────────────────────────────
PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
────────────────────────────────────────

Create a professional, structured document that:

1. Clearly explains what the product does
2. Defines who it is for
3. Describes the problem it solves
4. Explains the value it delivers
5. Positions MTE Software Ltd as the technology builder and operator

────────────────────────────────────────
OUTPUT FORMAT (STRICT)
────────────────────────────────────────

Return a single markdown document using the exact structure below.
No emojis.
No exaggerated claims.
No speculative roadmap features.
Be precise and commercially clear.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1 — PRODUCT OVERVIEW
────────────────────────────────────────

• Product Name
• Built and operated by: MTE Software Ltd
• Product category (e.g. SaaS platform, AI-assisted tool, marketplace, compliance system, etc.)
• One-paragraph executive summary explaining what the platform does and its primary purpose

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2 — CORE FUNCTIONALITY
────────────────────────────────────────

List the platform’s key capabilities grouped logically.

For each capability include:
• What it does
• Who uses it
• The outcome it produces
• Whether it is core, supporting, or administrative

Focus on operational capability, not UI detail.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3 — PROBLEM STATEMENT
────────────────────────────────────────

Define:
• The primary problem(s) the product solves
• The inefficiencies or risks it reduces
• The gap in the market it addresses

Base this on actual functionality, not aspiration.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 4 — TARGET MARKET
────────────────────────────────────────

Define clearly:

• Primary target users (roles, industries, business size)
• Secondary users (if applicable)
• Geographic scope (if relevant)
• Regulatory or operational context (if applicable)

Be specific and realistic.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 5 — IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)
────────────────────────────────────────

Describe:

• Organisation type
• Size or maturity level
• Pain points
• Decision maker profile
• Budget sensitivity (low, medium, high)
• Technical capability expectations

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 6 — VALUE PROPOSITION
────────────────────────────────────────

Explain:

• Why this product exists
• What measurable benefit it delivers
• Time, cost, risk, or efficiency impact
• How it improves decision-making or operational performance

Avoid generic claims.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 7 — COMPETITIVE POSITIONING
────────────────────────────────────────

Without naming specific competitors unless clearly applicable, describe:

• What makes this product structurally different
• Where it has operational depth
• Whether it competes on compliance, automation, usability, integration, intelligence, or cost

Only describe advantages that are supported by real functionality.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 8 — DELIVERY & OWNERSHIP
────────────────────────────────────────

State clearly:

• The product is built, maintained, and operated by MTE Software Ltd
• MTE Software Ltd controls the architecture and roadmap
• The platform is developed using modern, scalable technology
• The company provides ongoing technical stewardship and iteration

Do not overstate scale or compliance unless implemented.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 9 — COMMERCIAL MODEL (IF APPLICABLE)
────────────────────────────────────────

If relevant, describe:

• Subscription model
• Usage-based pricing
• Licensing structure
• Enterprise or partner deployment options

If pricing is not implemented, state that the commercial model is configurable.

────────────────────────────────────────
CONSTRAINTS
────────────────────────────────────────

• Do NOT invent features
• Do NOT reference future roadmap items
• Do NOT exaggerate
• Do NOT use promotional language
• Ensure MTE Software Ltd is clearly positioned as builder and operator
• Base all statements on actual platform capability

The final document must be suitable for direct inclusion in commercial materials.
